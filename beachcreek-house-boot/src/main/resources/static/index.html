Less than 5 minutes walk from the beach, this newly furnished single-family home is located on 2 secluded wooded acres with a spring-fed creek running alongside. Association beach has two access points, one with only 5 stairs for easy access. Located in Michigan Shores, tucked away behind the trees of Grand Beach, and next to Michiana beaches, this home provides privacy in a natural setting with abundant wildlife.
